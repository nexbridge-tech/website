Who We Are

NexBridge Technology connects Vietnamese manufacturers, OEMs and system integrators with the right global technology partners — from semiconductor suppliers like TI and NXP to materials specialists like T-Global — to solve real engineering problems.

Rather than pushing products, we start with the problem: understanding the application, the operating conditions and the constraints, then matching it to a component, material or partner that actually fits.

Drawing on hands-on experience from the front lines of Vietnam's electric vehicle industry, we bring practical, application-tested knowledge to every recommendation we make.

Our Mission

To empower Vietnamese industries by connecting them with the right global technologies to solve real engineering problems.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted engineering bridge between global technology innovators and Vietnamese industry.

Our Approach

Understand – Connect – Integrate – Deliver – Support.

Our Commitment

Integrity, partnership and long-term value creation for our customers and communities.
